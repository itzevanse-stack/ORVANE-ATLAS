The Model

Premium Property.
Fractional Ownership.

Orvane Atlas identifies, verifies, and structures premium properties for fractional investment. Multiple qualified investors pool capital to acquire one asset — each holding a legally documented share proportionate to their investment.

01
Property Identified
Orvane Atlas sources and fully verifies a premium property. Title, legal status, and market valuation confirmed before any investor is approached.
02
Shares Structured
The total property value is divided into equal shares. Minimum investment set per opportunity. Each share represents a proportionate ownership stake.
03
Investors Commit
Qualified investors commit their shares. Capital held in escrow until the pool is complete. Full transparency on all co-investors and structure.
04
Asset Acquired
Once fully subscribed, the property is acquired. Each investor receives legal documentation of their ownership share. Orvane Atlas manages the asset.

Why Fractional

The Advantages

Invest in high-value properties that would otherwise require significantly more capital. A ₦450M asset becomes accessible from ₦45M.

Legally Documented
Every investor receives proper legal documentation of their ownership share. Your investment is formally protected under Nigerian property law.

Rental income from the property is distributed proportionately among investors on a quarterly basis, net of management fees.

Capital Growth
As the property appreciates in value, each investor's share increases in value proportionately. Exit opportunities arranged by Orvane Atlas.

All investor capital is held in escrow until the property pool is fully subscribed. If the pool does not fill, capital is returned in full.
